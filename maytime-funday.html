Maytime Funday

On the 28th May The Friends of Trenance Gardens invite you to a colourful combo of charity stalls, fairground rides, games and entertainment down at Trenance Gardens. With live music and dancing, BBQ,bouncy castle, inflatable sumo wrestling and the famous duck racing, all the family is set for a fun day!!

The park is free to enter and is a charity event in support of the Trenance Cottages Project
